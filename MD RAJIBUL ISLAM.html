Frequently Asked Questions

What are your rates?

Pricing varies based on project complexity, duration, and specific requirements. Basic video editing starts at $50 per minute of final content. AI services start at $30 per task. Contact me with your project details for an accurate quote.

How many revisions do you allow?

I include 2 rounds of revisions in my standard pricing. Additional revisions can be arranged at an hourly rate. For AI services, we provide unlimited iterations until you're satisfied with the output.

What's your typical turnaround time?

For a standard 10-minute video, turnaround is 3-5 business days. Rush services are available for an additional fee. AI services typically deliver within 24-48 hours depending on complexity.

What file formats do you deliver?

Final videos are delivered in MP4 (H.264) format by default, but I can provide any format you need (MOV, AVI, ProRes, etc.). For AI services, we provide standard image formats (JPG, PNG) or specified formats.

Who owns the copyright to the final product?

You retain full rights to all final deliverables. I only request permission to include the work in my portfolio unless otherwise specified. For AI-generated content, all rights are transferred to you upon payment.
